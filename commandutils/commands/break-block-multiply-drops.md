# Break Block Multiply Drops

Usage: /breakblockmultiplydrops \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \<Player> \<Drops Multiplier>

* World - The world the location is in
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
* Player - The player to affect
* Drops Multiplier - Number to multiply the block's drops by
