# Silent Particle

Usage: /silentparticle \<Particle> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \[\<X Offset>] \[\<Y Offset>] \[\<Z Offset>] \[\<Speed>] \[\<Amount>] \[\<Force>]

* Particle - The particle to display
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
* X Offset _(optional)_ - Random X spread of the particles
* Y Offset _(optional)_ - Random Y spread of the particles
* Z Offset _(optional)_ - Random Z spread of the particles
* Speed _(optional)_ - Speed of the particles
* Amount _(optional)_ - Numerical amount
* Force _(optional)_ - Whether particles are shown to all players regardless of distance
