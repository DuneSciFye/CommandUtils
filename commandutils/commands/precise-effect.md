# Precise Effect

Usage: /preciseeffect \<Entities> \<Effect> \[\<Duration>] \[\<Level>] \[\<Hide Particles>] \[\<Ambient>]

Usage: /preciseeffect \<Entities> \<Effect> \[\<infinite>] \[\<Level>] \[\<Hide Particles>] \[\<Ambient>]

* Entities - The entities to affect (supports selectors)
* Effect - The potion effect
* Duration _(optional)_ - Duration, e.g. 5s, 1m, 100t
* Level _(optional)_ - The effect level / amplifier
* Hide Particles _(optional)_ - Whether to hide the effect particles
* Ambient _(optional)_ - Whether the effect is ambient
