# Spawn No Damage Firework

Usage: /spawnnodamagefirework \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \<Ticks To Detonate> \[\<No Damage Player>]

* World - The world the location is in
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
* Ticks To Detonate - Ticks before the firework detonates
* No Damage Player _(optional)_ - The player who should take no damage from the firework
