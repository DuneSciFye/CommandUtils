# Push Entity

Usage: /pushentity \<Entities> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \[\<Multiplier>]

Usage: /pushentity \<Entities> \<Target> \[\<Multiplier>]

* Entities - The entities to affect (supports selectors)
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
* Multiplier _(optional)_ - The multiplier to apply
* Target - The target entity/player
