# Mob Drops

Usage: /mobdrops \<set | add> \<Entity> \<Material List>

Usage: /mobdrops \<set2 | add2> \<Entity> \<Command Separator> \<Drops>

Usage: /mobdrops \<clear | clearcustom | clearcustomdrops | novanilladrops> \<Entity>

* Entity - The entity to affect
* Material List - List of materials
* Command Separator - The string used to separate individual commands
* Drops - The drops to give, separated by the command separator
