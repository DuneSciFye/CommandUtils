# Remove In Radius

Usage: /removeinradius \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \<Radius> \<Player>

Usage: /removeinradius \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \<Radius> \<Player> \<Whitelisted Blocks>

* World - The world the location is in
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
* Radius - Radius of the effect
* Player - The player to affect
* Whitelisted Blocks - Block predicate / whitelist of blocks that may be affected. Accepts a config-defined predicate name
