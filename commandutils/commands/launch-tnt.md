# Launch TNT

Usage: /launchtnt \<Player> \[\<Break Blocks>]

Usage: /launchtnt \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \[\<Break Blocks>]

* Player - The player to affect
* Break Blocks _(optional)_ - Whether the explosion breaks blocks
* World - The world the location is in
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
