# Get Player Head

Usage: /getplayerhead \<Player> \[\<Target>]

Usage: /getplayerhead \<Player> \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)>

* Player - The player to affect
* Target _(optional)_ - The target entity/player
* World - The world the location is in
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
