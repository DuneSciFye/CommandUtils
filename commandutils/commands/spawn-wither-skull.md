# Spawn Wither Skull

Usage: /spawnwitherskull \<[world](../arguments/world-argument.md)> \<[location](../arguments/block-location-argument.md)> \[\<Yaw>] \[\<Pitch>] \[\<Velocity Multiplier>] \[\<Break Blocks>]

* World - The world the location is in
* Location - Coordinates of the block/location
* Yaw _(optional)_ - Yaw rotation
* Pitch _(optional)_ - Pitch rotation
* Velocity Multiplier _(optional)_ - Multiplier applied to the velocity
* Break Blocks _(optional)_ - Whether the explosion breaks blocks
