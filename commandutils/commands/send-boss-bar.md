# Send Boss Bar

Usage: /sendbossbar \<Player> \<Bossbar ID> \<Bossbar Color> \<Bossbar Progress> \<Ticks To Show> \<Bossbar Content>

* Player - The player to affect
* Bossbar ID - Unique identifier for the boss bar
* Bossbar Color - Colour of the boss bar
* Bossbar Progress - Progress of the boss bar (0.0-1.0)
* Ticks To Show - How many ticks to display the boss bar
* Bossbar Content - The boss bar text
