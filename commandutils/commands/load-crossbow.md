# Load Crossbow

Usage: /loadcrossbow \<Player> \<[slot](../arguments/slot-argument.md)> \[\<Loaded>] \[\<Interact With Inventory>]

* Player - The player to affect
* Slot - Slot of the item
* Loaded _(optional)_ - Whether the crossbow should be loaded
* Interact With Inventory _(optional)_ - Whether to consume ammo from the inventory
