# Launch Firework

Usage: /launchfirework \<Player> \<Ticks To Detonate> \<No Damage> \<RGB> \[\<Firework Power>]

Usage: /launchfirework \<Player> \[\<Ticks To Detonate>] \[\<No Damage>] \[\<Red RGB>] \[\<Green RGB>] \[\<Blue RGB>] \[\<Firework Power>]

* Player - The player to affect
* Ticks To Detonate - Ticks before the firework detonates
* No Damage - Whether the firework deals no damage
* RGB - Packed RGB colour value
* Firework Power _(optional)_ - Firework flight power
* Red RGB _(optional)_ - Red colour component (0-255)
* Green RGB _(optional)_ - Green colour component (0-255)
* Blue RGB _(optional)_ - Blue colour component (0-255)

> Aliases: `spawnfirework`, `summonfirework`.
