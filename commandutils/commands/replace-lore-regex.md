# Replace Lore Regex

Usage: /replaceloreregex \<Player> \<[slot](../arguments/slot-argument.md)> \<Text To Find> \<New Text>

* Player - The player to affect
* Slot - Slot of the item
* Text To Find - The text to search for
* New Text - The replacement text
