# Copy Effects

Usage: /copyeffects \<Entities To Copy From> \<Potion Types> \[\<Amplifier>] \[\<Duration>] \[\<Remove Effect From Targets>] \[\<Ignore Infinite Duration Effects>] \[\<Max Number of Effects>]

* Entities To Copy From - The entities whose effects are copied
* Potion Types - List of potion effect types
* Amplifier _(optional)_ - Effect amplifier (0 = level I)
* Duration _(optional)_ - Duration, e.g. 5s, 1m, 100t
* Remove Effect From Targets _(optional)_ - Whether to remove the copied effects from the source entities
* Ignore Infinite Duration Effects _(optional)_ - Whether to skip effects with infinite duration
* Max Number of Effects _(optional)_ - Maximum number of effects to copy
